Services a Professional Plumber Should Offer

A qualified plumber should provide comprehensive services, including:

Questions to Ask Before Hiring

Before making your decision, ask potential plumbers:

  • Do you offer 24/7 emergency services?
  • What warranties do you provide?
  • Can you provide local references?
  • What's your response time for emergencies?
  • Do you charge flat rates or hourly?

Red Flags to Watch For

Avoid plumbers who:

  • Cannot provide proper licensing
  • Offer significantly lower prices than competitors
  • Refuse to provide written estimates
  • Have no local presence or references
  • Pressure you into immediate decisions
